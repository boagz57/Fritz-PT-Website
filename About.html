About Us

Here at Fritz Physical Therapy we pride ourselves on providing exceptional treatment to everyone who comes through our doors. But even more than that, we pride ourselves on treating our patients as human beings and not just another number. We provide a friendly, fun and professional environment for people of all ages who need physical therapy.

For the uninitiated, the services we provide revolve around the most up to date physical therapy practices which include unique exercise perscription tailored to individual needs, ultrasound, electrical stimulation (STIM), manual manipulation, traction and more. We do not provide general massage or chiropractic services. For any questions please be sure to give us a call at
412-278-1221!
